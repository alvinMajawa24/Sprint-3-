WELCOME TO THE BUS STATION KENYA

More info

The Central Bus Station, situated on Temple Road between Mfangano Street and Uyoma Street, was for a long time reserved for the exclusive use of Kenya Bus Service(KBS), the oldest public transport company in Nairobi which at one time had a monopoly on intra-city mass transport services in Nairobi and Mombasa.

In the mid 1980's, Nyayo Bus Service was introduced, and the smaller 14-seater Matatus started appearing on the scene, bringing to an end the long running KBS monopoly.
While the Nyayo Bus Service has since collapsed, other bus companies,other bus companies have emerged to take its place.

Matatus in this stage usually cover the following routes:

  • South B
  • South C
  • Nairobi West/Madaraka
  • Highrise/Raila Odinga Road
  • Kitengela
  • Umoja
  • Ruai-Joska
